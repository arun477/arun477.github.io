### What is the “Scientific Method”?
<img src="/assets/feynman.png" width="300">

##### 1. Guess
Just guess, what might be the reason for the specific behavior when you observe it.

##### 2. Consequences
If your guess is correct, list out all the consequences.

##### 3. Experiment
Simply, check if all the consequences for your guess occur during the experiment, if it doesn’t agree with the experiment then your guess is incorrect, if it agrees then your guess is not wrong yet!

##### Example: Law of Gravity
*Newton*: Hmm, I guess every object attracts every other object.

*Newton*: If my guess is correct then we can use this guess to predict the dynamics of the planets.

*Joe*: Ok let me calculate the dynamics using your guess.

Joe: Cool, it predicts correctly!

...: Cool, it predicts correctly!

…: Cool, it predicts correctly!

…: Cool, it predicts correctly!

*Jenny*: Hey, but it predicts wrongly for the planet mercury dynamics, so your guess is wrong.

*Einstein*: Ok then, **I guess…**.
